    WORKOUT

    To start with, we’ll be taking a look at a beginner workout routine. This workout isn’t too difficult; though, for those new to health and fitness,
    it will certainly prove challenging.

    Chest

    Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets of 8 reps.

    Back

    Lat-pulldowns – 4 sets of 10 reps.

    Shoulders

    Seated Dumbbell Press – 4 sets of 10 reps.

    Legs

    Leg Extensions – 4 sets of 10 reps.
